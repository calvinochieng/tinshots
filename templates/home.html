Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers.

Who Am I? +

I’m Martin Edwards , a passionate professional photographer dedicated to capturing life’s special moments. Whether it’s a grand event or an intimate family session, my goal is to deliver stunning photos that you’ll cherish forever. My style combines creativity, attention to detail, and a commitment to exceeding your expectations.

Where Are Photoshoots Held? +

Photoshoots can be held at a location of your choice! Whether you prefer the comfort of your home, a scenic outdoor venue, or my private studio, I work with you to create the perfect setting for your shoot. For events like birthdays, baby showers, and weddings, I come to the event venue to ensure no moment is missed.

How Much Do Photoshoots Cost? +

The cost of a photoshoot depends on the type of session and location. Studio sessions and private events at your home or chosen venue may vary in price. Feel free to contact me for a personalized quote based on your unique needs and preferences. The rate and pricing are detialed on the pricing page

How Will I Receive My Photos? +

Photos are delivered through a private Google Photos album, ensuring a secure and convenient way to access your images. You’ll receive a personalized link that you can easily download or share with family and friends.

What Are Your Working Hours? +

My working hours are flexible and depend on the type of session or event. Whether it’s a daytime studio session or an evening event at your venue, I’m committed to working around your schedule to make sure your experience is seamless.

Do You Offer Private Event Photography? +

Absolutely! I’m happy to cover private events such as birthdays, baby showers, baby bump sessions, and more. These shoots can be held at your home or a location of your choosing. Please note that pricing may vary based on the type of event and location.
